Steps to Book

FOLLOW THESE STEPS

  • Step 1: On the menu, click Book a Room and select the hostel block you want to book.
  • Step 2: Select the corresponding floor you want to book.
  • Step 3: Select a vacant room and click on proceed.
  • Step 4: Fill in your information and click Generate Invoice.
  • Note: Once a room is selected, you have 24 hours to make payment; otherwise, the room will be unlocked for another person to book.
  • Step 5: Click Pay Now and choose your preferred payment method:
    • Pay With Card — select this option and complete the transaction using your debit/credit card.
    • Pay With Bank Transfer — select this option and follow the instructions for a direct transfer.
    • Pay in a Bank Branch — select this option, then click Print Invoice to download a PDF copy. Take the printed invoice to one of the designated banks to complete your payment.
  • Step 6: Return to the portal and go to Process → Complete Application Form.
  • Step 7: Enter your invoice number (format: ELCOOU***) to proceed.
  • Step 8: Complete your registration and print your application form.
