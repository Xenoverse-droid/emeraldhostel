FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is Emerald Hostel located?

Emerald Hostel is located in Nnamdi Azikiwe University (Unizik), Awka, Anambra State.

Does it have self-contained rooms?

No. The rooms are shared.

Are the rooms single or shared?

The rooms are shared. There are 3 students per room.

Is the hostel highly secured?

The rooms are secured since the hostel is within the school compound.

How do I apply for a hostel space?

Click on the "APPLY NOW" button and follow the procedures that appear.

Online payment?

Yes. Select your bank, enter account number, then enter the OTP code sent to the phone number associated with the bank.
